About Us

Our story begins when our founder Mr Kunhumohamed boarded a boat from a small village in India to UAE 50 years back. From this humble beginning , we have grown to become the largest wholesaler in the UAE and a holding group with an annual turnover of AED 1.8+ billion. As pioneers in cash and carry model in the UAE, we are trusted name in the industry dealing with genuine good quality products. Our premium clientele include Dubai Police, Dubai Customs, Sharjah police, Etisalat among others.

Our Customers

We have 12000+ happy customers. Our customers include groceries, supermarkets, Government bodies, institutions, trading firms, cafeterias, restaurants and Hotels. We provide instore service as well as delivery to our customers

Warehousing

We deliver across UAE with 75 trucks and have a total storage space of over 200,000+ sqft. All our stores and warehouses are HACCP certified and uses latest technologies for logistics

Products

We deal with 6000+ products in 170+ categories. We have extensive assortment of grocery, frozen and FMCG products in both bulk and consumer packing. We strive in delivering good quality genuine products at competitive rates.

Turnover
